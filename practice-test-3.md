### 第四部分：英译汉（共 15 分）

1.  **A computer network is a group of interconnected computers that can share resources and data with each other.**
    -   翻译：计算机网络是一组相互连接的计算机，它们可以彼此共享资源和数据。
2.  **Software engineering is the application of engineering principles to the development, maintenance, and testing of computer software.**
    -   翻译：软件工程是将工程原理应用于计算机软件的开发、维护和测试。
3.  **The main function of an operating system is to manage the computer's hardware resources and provide a platform for application software.**
    -   翻译：操作系统的主要功能是管理计算机的硬件资源，并为应用软件提供一个平台。
