## **第四部分答案**

1.  **Modern cloud platforms automate resource provisioning to ensure efficient scaling and reduce manual configuration efforts.**
    -   现代云平台通过自动化资源供应来确保高效扩展，并减少人工配置的工作量。
2.  **The reliability of a distributed system depends heavily on its ability to tolerate node failures and maintain consistent data.**
    -   分布式系统的可靠性在很大程度上取决于其容忍节点故障并保持数据一致性的能力。
3.  **Encryption techniques protect sensitive information by converting plaintext into unreadable ciphertext during transmission and storage.**
    -   加密技术通过在传输和存储过程中将明文转换为不可读的密文来保护敏感信息。
